PS5 is more expensive in many countries; Will Brazil be affected?
Despite Brazil not appearing on the list, Sony said the PS5's value could increase in select Latin American markets. However, the company confirmed that Brazil is off the list of readjustments.
Maykon Lafaette 12/05/2019
Playstation 5, console of the new generation of video games. (Photo: Sony)
The PlayStation 5 is expensive, but it will get even more expensive in different parts of the world. In a statement, Sony Interactive Entertainment President Jim Ryan announced the increase in select markets. The reasons range from high inflation rate, high dollar and adverse currency trends. Therefore, in many different locations, the console will be increased between $30 and $80 at retail.
The price change is officially active around the world, however, select markets in EMEA, APAC and LATAM not listed above may also receive increases. That is, it will be necessary to check with each store and seller. This means that Brazil is not totally free of this increase.
Sony president in the announcement of the new prices.
"Based on challenging economic conditions, SIE has made the difficult decision to increase the PlayStation 5 Recommended Retail Price (RRP) in select markets in Europe, Middle East and Africa (EMEA), Asia Pacific (APAC), Latin America ( LATAM) as well as Canada. We were thinking and analyzing the market and decided that this would be the best solution." Said the president of Sony.
